Sweet and Sour Chicken

Chicken with sweet and sour sauce, pineapple, and peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tblsp coconut oil or olive oil
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 Tblsp soy sauce
  • 4 Tblsp cornstarch
  • 3 medium size bell peppers
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 20 ounces canned pineapple chunks drained with liquid reserved
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 Tblsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1-4 Tblsp siracha hot sauce
  • Cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken (cut into strips) in a bowl and toss with 1 tablespoon soy sauce. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of cornstarch and toss to coat. In a separate bowl, whisk together the pineapple liquid, water, vinegar, brown sugar, remaining soy sauce, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a very large skillet over high heat. Add the chicken and toss quickly to coat. Continue tossing for 2-3 minutes. Add the peppers and the snap peas and continue stirring and tossing fro 2 more minutes. Lower the heat if needed.
  3. Increase the heat to high again and add the pineapple chunks and the sauce. Cook, stirring constantly for about 2 minutes, until the sauce is thick and sticky. Taste the sauce and add the siracha a tablespooon at a time. Stirring well, between each addition.
  4. Serve over rice
